Bangla Phonetic — Typing Guide

Type romanized Bangla, then press Space to commit a word. Switch Bangla/English with Ctrl+Shift+B.

The scheme is case-sensitive: capitals are different letters (t = ত but T = ট).

Vowels

A vowel at the start of a word is an independent vowel; right after a consonant it becomes a vowel sign (kar) — shown on ক (k).
